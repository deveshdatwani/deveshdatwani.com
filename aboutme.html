With three generations of engineers in my family, curiosity was the natural mood of my home.

I embarked on the journey of Robotics in high school when I started building remotely controlled fixed wing aircaft to satiate my passion for aviation. Learning about electronics and flight dynamics involved, I started building fixed-wing RC planes from scratch. The process of building RC planes and watch them take flight built my passion-filled foundation for a career in engineering.


To fortify my hobby-turned-passion, I pursued undergraduate studies in Mechanical Engineering at Mumbai University where I was awared the young innovator award for my work on Plasma Actuators. The curriculum of my undergraduate studies involved subjects like mechatronics, machine design, structured programming approach and industrial electronics.


I signed up for a Python Programming course right after my undergraduate studeis which I can say with conviction was a pivot point in defining my career path. The idea of automating robots to improve quality of life fills me up with excitement.


Robotics, Python and Machine Learning bridge the gap between my hobby and passion, which is to build autonomous vehicles.
