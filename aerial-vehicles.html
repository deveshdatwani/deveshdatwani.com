Aerial Vehicles

Since the summer of 2011, I have been building aerial vehicles as a means to satiate my passion for aviation. Over the years, my projects have included fixed winged rc planes of varying configurations, quadcopters and more.


I gained hands on experience while building them. While most of them were powered by brushless motors, some were powered by nitro-methane powered IC engines.


I will keep this page straighforward and put out snapshots of the aerial vehicles I built. Please note, these are only some of the drones/planes I built. Not all were documented.
