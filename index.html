I embarked on the journey of Robotics back in high school, when I started building RC planes for hobby. Reading about flight dynamics and electronics, I gathered enough knowledge to start building fixed wing RC aircraft. Nonetheless, it took me more than two prototypes to get a stable flight. Dramatic as it sounds, the process of building something and see it take flight gives me a high that cannot be matched with anything. The process of building, creating, innovating is my Ikegai.

To fortify my hobby-turned-passion with formal training, I pursued undergraduate studies in Mechanical Engineering, which helped me build a strong foundation in Robotics. My curriculum involved studies in subjects such as mechatronics, machine design, C programming, and industrial electronics.

I am currently an incoming student of Ms in Robotics at the Worcester Polytechnic Institute, MA, United States of America.

Ps- I volunteer for non-profit organisations in whichever way I can. I volunteered to teach Science to students of lesser priveleged backgrounds for a two years (2018-2020) under the organisation Spreading Smiles. If anyone readin this has anything I could help with, please free to drop me an email.
